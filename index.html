PLACE TO LIVE

Place to live The town of Saint Vlas is conveniently located at the eastern part of the foot of the Balkan Mountains on the Black Sea coast. Sveti Vlas resort city is a very successful location due to its unique climate combining the purity of mountain air and the uniqueness of sea breezes. The life-giving and healing mixture of iodine-enriched sea and crystal-clear mountain air is indispensable for the treatment of diseases of the upper respiratory tract. Suffering from bronchial asthma and other lung diseases immediately feel an improvement, inhaling such a natural medicine, which allowed Sveti Vlas to gain a reputation as a spa resort. Excellent sandy beaches, with a total length of 1.5 kilometers, will delight anyone, even the most discerning traveler.

DEVELOPED INFRASTRUCTURE

Developed infrastructure In the immediate vicinity of the complex, within walking distance, there are shops, ATM, banks, restaurants, playgrounds. There is always the opportunity to rent a yacht and take a boat trip, visit the tasting of Bulgarian wines and products. Sveti Vlas is located just 5 kilometers north of the most famous Bulgarian Black Sea holiday destination - Sunny Beach, 9 kilometers from ancient Nessebar and, not least, just 40 kilometers from Bourgas International Airport.

PROFITABLE INVESTMENT

Profitable investment In addition to acquiring a private apartment at a fashionable Black Sea resort, eliminating the need to search and book hotel rooms at the height of the holiday season, the property owner has the opportunity to earn income from his investment by renting an apartment. And do not forget that since 2007 Bulgaria is part of the European Union.

ARCHITECTURE

Architecture The architecture of the complex meets all international requirements for energy efficiency. The complex is built on an individual project using the most modern technologies and the highest quality materials. It lacks long corridors and therefore you have only a few neighbors. The facades of the building will be lined with the highest quality natural materials including natural stone and marble.

INTERIOR

Interior Many apartments have large terraces and create a romantic atmosphere. Most apartments allow you to admire not only a fantastic view of the sea, but also a mountain landscape. For residents with small children or pets, it is possible to choose apartments with a green area under the window and their own separate exit.
